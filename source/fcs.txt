All MS students are expected to have completed the undergraduate
prerequisites:

- :doc:`comp388fcs1` is an accelerated version of :doc:`comp163` and :doc:`comp170`
- :doc:`comp388fcs2` is an accelerated version of :doc:`comp264` and :doc:`comp271`

Please note that MS IT students are expected to complete *all* prerequisites
before taking any course in CS or the Quinlan School of Business. (This includes any additional
prerequisites required by Quinlan.)
